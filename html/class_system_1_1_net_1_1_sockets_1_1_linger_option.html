Detailed Description

Specifies whether a T:System.Net.Sockets.Socket will remain connected after a call to the M:System.Net.Sockets.Socket.Close or M:System.Net.Sockets.TcpClient.Close methods and the length of time it will remain connected, if data remains to be sent.
