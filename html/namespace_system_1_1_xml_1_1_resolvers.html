enum System.Xml.Resolvers.XmlKnownDtds
strong

The T:System.Xml.Resolvers.XmlKnownDtds enumeration is used by the T:System.Xml.Resolvers.XmlPreloadedResolver and defines which well-known DTDs that the T:System.Xml.Resolvers.XmlPreloadedResolver recognizes.

Enumerator
None 

Specifies that the T:System.Xml.Resolvers.XmlPreloadedResolver will not recognize any of the predefined DTDs.

Xhtml10 

Specifies that the T:System.Xml.Resolvers.XmlPreloadedResolver will recognize DTDs and entities that are defined in XHTML 1.0.

Rss091 

Specifies that the T:System.Xml.Resolvers.XmlPreloadedResolver will recognize DTDs and entities that are defined in RSS 0.91.

All 

Specifies that the T:System.Xml.Resolvers.XmlPreloadedResolver will recognize all currently supported DTDs. This is the default behavior.
